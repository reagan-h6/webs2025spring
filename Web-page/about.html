About Reagan

Reagan began her academic career in the hidden valleys of Vernon, NJ where she attended Vernon Township High school. She was an active member of the community, volunteering for local organizations such PAL where she was a camp conselor and VTYS where she coached a U8 girls and boys soccer team. She graduated with honor and in the top 10% of her class, with an impressive transcript behind her she was ready to begin her college journey. She was thrilled to be accepted into the University of Pittsburgh where she planned to spend her undergraduate years. In an interview with Reagan she described Pitt as "the obvious choice" as soon as she stepped on the campus for the first time. Her first semester at Pitt she rushed to join clubs that interested her, she spent most her time volunteering with students for sustainability and playing with the Pitt club soccer team. Her first introduction to her love for research came when she became a undergraduate teaching assistant (UTA) for the duckweed survivor lab.
